Pelé

A list of Pelé's greatest moments:

  • Oct 23, 1940 - Pelé was born in Tres Coracoes, Brazil.
  • Sep 7, 1956 - Pelé was 16 when he joined the Brazilian soccer team, Santos.
  • June 19, 1958 - Pelé became the youngest player to play and win a World Cup final. He scored 2 goals.
  • June 17, 1962 - Pelé won his second World Cup with Brazil.
  • May 31, 1970 - Pelé won his third World Cup with Brazil, becoming the only player to win it three times.
  • July 18, 1971 - Pelé played his last international match against Yugoslavia.
  • June 10, 1975 - Pelé joined the New York Cosmos. He was credited with making soccer popular in North America.
  • Oct 1, 1977 - Pelé retires from soccer, where he played his final game against his boyhood club Santos.
